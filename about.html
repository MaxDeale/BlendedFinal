About Us

Blended Audio is based in the Bo-Kaap, Tamboerskloof area in Cape Town


Currently our studios consist out of 7 fully fledged dubbing suites, one foley suite and stage, 2 Mixing Control Rooms, one of which will soon be a 5.1 suite.

We have been doing work for various clients over the last 5 years, including Zee Tv, Prime Focus, Native ninja, and many more. Primarily for Zee Tv Africa.


Blended Audio used to be a small department at The Waterfront Film Studios , however we have now become independent from The Waterfront Film Studios when the facility was sold over to Silverline Studios, and we moved and built new studios in tamboerskloof.


We are a deadline driven company with 5 years of experience in dealing with huge quantities of content at a time on tight deadlines, and delivering top quality work.


Our facility can at full capacity push out 168 hours of content per month if all 7 studios are working at full force, and this can be increased if need be by using the foley studio as a dubbing booth. Currently we are running 7 studios from 8am to 11 pm to achieve total of 6 play out hours of content per week per studio.

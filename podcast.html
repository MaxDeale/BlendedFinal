Blended Podcast

Blended Podcasts was founded by Pieter Matthyser, Executive Producer at Blended Audio to provide a platform for people who have the enthusiasm, but no access to professional recording studios. We are starting off with 6 shows, daily releases from Monday to Saturday, these shows will change over time according to their engagement, audience and of course the availability of the hosts.
